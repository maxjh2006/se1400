Why MAC'S?

MACS has a rich history, one of grit, detirmination, hard work, and success; being built from the ground up we have amassed dedicated staff who deliver perfection, and we have curated the single most experienced, successful, and influential gym in Utah.



Where Does Macs Compete?

We compete many times over the season, in state at local competitions, and out of state several times for super nationals. This past season our teams competed 2-5 times locally, along with 2 times at a high school for friends and family, and a handful of out of state competitions in places like Dalls, Orlando, Indianapolis, LA, Atlanta, and Las Vegas.

What do you Offer at Macs?

We offer all levels of regular all star cheer, tiny, prep, mini, youth, junior, senior and open level teams. We have lessons all year round, stunting, tumbling, and flexibility. We want your child to become the best athlete they can possibly be!



What is All Star Cheer?

All star cheer is a sport defined by three things, stunting, tumbling, and performing. We teach discipline, leaderhsip, and all of these aforementioned skills in order to build a bond between teamates; strength, and dedication in all. We compete locally and nationally several times each season, which cycles every 11 months, making this a year round sport. It takes a lot of time and effort, but is worth your every minute.

Who is This for?

We offer a range of classes, teams, and groups in order to fit all age and skill groups. We offer levels 1 through 6, ages 3-19 are able to compete most levels based on skills; and we offer private and group lessons for all ages to improve their abilities. We also have an open team, Legacy, which is available to those who age above the regular brackets. We offer things for all ages who are willing to try!

What Does Your Gym Like?

Located in Draper, Utah, our facility has 3 full sized spring floors, a large space with cooling and heating, outside access and spacious parking. We have an office and parent viewing area where you can find the front desk, and backpack storage areas.
