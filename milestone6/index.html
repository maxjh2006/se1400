What We offer

We offer a variety of classes and teams; tumbling, stunting, and teaching discipline to benefit your children all year round! We are accepting of all skills, and work to fit all ages and types into our teams! We'd love to have you join us!

Our History

Our founders, Mack and Janet, have been running Macs Cheer for over 20 years, being in the industry for even longer, these experts have been working hard for a very long time. We have gone through multiple gym locations, teams, and staff. All this to say we know what we are doing, we have been doing it, and have defined and refined all star cheerleading in Utah.
