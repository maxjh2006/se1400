Teams, Levels, and Camps

All Star Cheer

We offer levels 1-6, including prep, non compete, and mini, youth, junior, senior, and open; we have even started to offer a half year team for a lighter commitment. There is the right fit for an athlete of any age, skill, or experience. Practice are evenings Monday through Friday, with occasional weekends.

Private Lessons

Our top tier coaches offer private, 1 on 1 lessons all year round, these help athletes refine and learn new skills, while improving their knowledge and abilities. This in a extra option but can prove very useful in moving up in level. There are options for both tumbling and stunting.

Group Tumbling Camps

Throughout the summer and during breaks we typically offer group tumbling clinics that drill and improve skills of all levels. These are announced on social media and through the gym Band. These are open to any and all athletes, whether active at Macs or not, we are here to help.
